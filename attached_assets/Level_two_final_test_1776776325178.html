Reading passage

Sarnai has been interested in art since she was a small child. She has always loved drawing, painting, and creating things with her hands. Last year, she decided to apply to the National Art School in Ulaanbaatar. Although she was very nervous about the entrance exam, she worked extremely hard for three months. She practised every single day, and her teacher told her that she had improved significantly. On the day of the exam, Sarnai arrived early and felt calm. She painted a landscape of the Mongolian steppe, which she loves more than any other subject. Two weeks later, she received a letter — she had been accepted! If she continues to work hard, she will become one of the best young artists in Mongolia. Sarnai says: "I have always believed that if you love something and dedicate yourself to it completely, you will achieve your dreams."
